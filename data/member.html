Your show

You are responsible to provide any special instructions on your artwork. ex. digital media, mechanical parts, interactive pices, if a piece needs to be watched, etc.

Install:

Clean bathroom and make sure there is supply of TP / soap / paper towls

Sweep and mop after INSTALL

Mops and brooms are located in the hallway closet

Pedestals are located upstairs and in the closet underneath the stairs

Bulbs upstairs. 90w floods and spots

Ladders out-back (keep door proped or you'll get locked out)

Tools and hardware upstairs (best to bring your own)

Opening Night:

Provide food and beverages

Have donations suitcase out (Phil collects nightly)

Make sure the guest book is out

Take out garbage AFTER opening (dumpster in alley)

Sitting:

Memebers two weeks Thursday - Sunday

Associates one week Thursday - Sunday

This can be worked out between the artists

Opening Space:

CLEAN BATHROOM

CLEAN PATIO

Put OUT open sign

Turn ON digital or mechanical art pices OR other special case art needs

Have phone with you

Check for messages on the phone, write a note or call to relay the information

Check the mail; open anything important and pass it on or trash it if it is junk mail

Keep stairway and phone area CLEAR of trash and items that create a tripping hazard

Organize the space between the two galleries; keep clean and remove any old invites laying around (includes mop room)

Closing Space:

Take IN open sign

Turn OFF digital or mechanical art pices OR other special case art needs

Hang up phone

Sweep or mop if necessary

Close ALL doors behind you

Un-Install:

Should be done within 48hrs of show close OR worked out with following artist

Clean bathroom and make sure there is supply of TP / soap / paper towls

Sweep and mop after UN-INSTALL

Putty holes, sand/sponge out putty

Paint any blemishes that may or may not have happened during show

Return pedestals to closet underneath the stairs

Return any gallery modifications back to 100% original state

Take out garbage (dumpster in alley)
